ABOUT THE EVENT

Welcome to ICNGCS 2021

The International Conference on Next Generation Computing Systems is one of the flagship conferences of the Institution, dedicated to young researchers and renowned scientists to come together and share their ideas about disruptions and future directions.
  • A three-day event.
  • Technical sessions featuring technical papers extensively reviewed by peers.
  • Workshops focusing on the latest trends in various technology.
  • Tutorials delivered by experts in respective disciplines
  • Technical papers, tutorials and workshop proposals accepted through a double blind review process.
  • Poster Presentations.
  • Exhibits.
